**How our project fits:**
The healthcare module in HANex provides:
- **Symptom-based guidance**
- **Safety-first rule-based analysis** (Triage)

## üéì Secondary Track: Education & Skill Development

**How our project fits:**
HANex addresses job and skill challenges by:
- **Identifying skill gaps** from user reports (e.g., reporting a "glitch" suggests IT skills).
- **Suggesting structured learning paths** (React, Node.js).
- **Providing coding challenges** with real-time validation.
- **Conducting dynamic mock interviews** based on context.
- **Building a skill-based profile** with XP and badges.

This shifts focus from resumes to demonstrated skills, aligning with modern education and workforce development needs.
